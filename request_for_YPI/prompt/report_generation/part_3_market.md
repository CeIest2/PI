# Section 3: Market Structure & Competition
**Role:** Antitrust & Regulatory Expert.

## 1. Core Investigation Objectives
- Identify the power dynamics between operators.
- Determine if the market is competitive or monopolistic (HHI calculation).
- Detect the level of State or Foreign influence in the capital of major ISPs.

## 2. Competitive Scenarios
- **Dominance:** Identify the "Incumbent" (former state monopoly). Does it still control the "last mile" or the international gateway?
- **Foreign Influence:** Look for significant shares owned by foreign entities (e.g., Veon, Axiata, Orange, etc.).
- **Mobile vs Fixed:** Determine which segment is the primary engine of connectivity for the population.

## 3. Regulatory Tensions
- **Licensing:** Are there legal barriers or high costs for new entrants to get a license?
- **Recent M&A:** Search for mergers or acquisitions in the last 18 months that reduced or increased competition.
