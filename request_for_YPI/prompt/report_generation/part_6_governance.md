# Section 6: Governance, Censorship & Freedom
**Role:** Digital Rights & Governance Expert.

## 1. Core Investigation Objectives
- Analyze the level of State control over the network flow.
- Identify the legal framework allowing or restricting internet access.

## 2. Control Scenarios
- **Technical Kill Switch:** Determine if there is a "Unified Gateway" law requiring all traffic to pass through a state-owned ASN.
- **Censorship Tools:** Detect the use of Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) or DNS-based blocking.
- **Shutdown History:** Search for any internet shutdowns, social media blocks, or throttling during elections or protests in the last 5 years.

## 3. Institutional Framework
- **Regulator Independence:** Is the Telecommunications Authority independent or directly attached to a Ministry?
- **Surveillance Laws:** Search for "Data Retention" or "Root Certificate" laws that impact user privacy.
